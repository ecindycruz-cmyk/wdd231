Site Foundations

Site Name

Name: Maybecr Expedition

This name represents a personalized adventure platform focused on El Salvador's hidden treasures. "Expedition" suggests exploration beyond common tourism.

Site Purpose

To highlight emerging destinations and small businesses, connecting travelers with local cooperatives and craftsmen tourism benefits.

User Scenarios

  • "Where can I find an authentic indigo workshop outside traditional zones?"
  • "Which culinary route is best to visit given today's weather?"

Branding & Style

Typography

Headings: Montserrat (Bold)
Selected for its modern and adventurous character.

Body: Open Sans (Regular)
Selected for high readability on all digital devices.

Color Scheme

#558B2F (Primary Green)

#3F6699 (Secondary Seawater)

#FF7043 (Accent Orange)

Visual Wireframe

A responsive layout featuring a hero section, followed by a grid of destination cards, and a footer with entrepreneur contact info.
